Privacy Policy

Last updated: 09-09-2025

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website, booking services, and communication platforms.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, payment details, ID/passport (if required for check-in).
  • Booking Information: Check-in/out dates, preferences, special requests.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device info, cookies.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process bookings and payments.
  • To communicate with you (confirmation, reminders, updates).
  • To improve our services and website.
  • For legal and security purposes.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe, Mobile Money, Crypto providers).
  • Booking form providers (e.g., Jotform).
  • Analytics & marketing tools (Google Analytics, social media).
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

Data Retention & Security

  • We store personal data only as long as necessary for business and legal requirements.
  • We use industry-standard encryption and secure servers to protect data.

Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
