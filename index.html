Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your stay with us

Our luxury apartments come fully furnished with premium amenities including high-speed WiFi, smart TVs, premium bedding, full kitchen appliances, and access to all building facilities such as the fitness center, parking lots, and swimming pool.

You can book directly through Booking.com or Airbnb for instant confirmation. For personalized assistance, contact us via WhatsApp (+233 59 899 3660) or bookings@truvelaapartments.com.

Our cancellation policy varies by booking platform and apartment type. Generally, we offer flexible cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in. Please refer to your booking confirmation for specific terms and conditions.

Each of our guests enjoys access to a designated parking bay for added convenience. Our concierge team will be delighted to assist you with tailored parking arrangements.

While transportation is not included as part of our services, we are delighted to assist with arrangements upon request. For airport transfers, local travel, or business trip needs, kindly notify us at least 48 hours prior to check-in, and our concierge will connect you with a trusted and secure service provider.

Our 24/7 concierge team can assist with restaurant reservations, transportation arrangements, local recommendations, housekeeping services, and any special requests to make your stay exceptional.
